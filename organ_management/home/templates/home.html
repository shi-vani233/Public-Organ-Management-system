How Donation Works

Time Frame for Transplanting Organs?

organs like the heart and lungs, can only remain viable for transplant after being outside of the body for 4 to 6 hours, while the liver can function for up to 12 hours and kidneys up to 36 hours.

What is the success rate of organ transplants?

A transplanted liver will function for five years or more in 70 percent of recipients, and even longer if the organ came from a living donor. After a heart transplant, the five-year survival rate for the organ is about 76 percent.

Who can be Skin donor?

Any person above age 16 will be accepted as skin donor after death if do not have Skin Cancer,skin infection or damaged skin.

what Living donors can potentially donate?

Living donors can donate One of two kidneys,One of two lobes of their liver,A lung or part of a lung, part of the pancreas, or part of the intestines.
